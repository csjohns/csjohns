Teaching

I have been the primary instructor for the undergraduate course Political Science as a Social Science

I am also currently a consultant at the Center for Social Science Computation and Research, where I develop and teach short training courses in software for research and statistical analysis (in particular R and ATLAS.ti). Short courses I have developed include: Introduction to R with R Studio, Data Wrangling in R, R Graphics with ggplot2, Introduction to Interactive Graphics in R, and Introduction to Qualitative Data Analysis with Atlas.ti.
